Link to your company's account

To start new projects or participate in existing ones, your account must be associated with a paid account that is linked to your company.

If no one at your company has a paid account

You can select a subscription plan and become the administrator of the Broadstat account associated with your organization. This will enable you to invite your colleagues to work with you, manage the subscription, and more.

If someone at your company already has a paid account

They can invite you to join the Broadstat account associated with your organization. All they need to do is ensure that the chosen subscription plan has room for an additional user and send you an invitation from their 'My Account' section.
